About Hanuman Entreprises

Hanuman Entreprises (Packseal House) is a Pune-based manufacturing company specializing in Pack Seal Machines used for food and industrial packaging.

We manufacture Hand Sealers, Foot Sealers, Automatic Conveyor Sealers and Toggle Sealers in multiple sizes and thickness options.

Our machines are widely used in bakeries, dry fruit packaging, milk packing, chips packing and many other industries requiring strong and reliable sealing.

We focus on durability, quality and affordable pricing while delivering machines in large quantities to meet daily market demand.
